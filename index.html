Kad's Media Ratings

Personal tier lists with ratings and short reviews

I often find myself stuck on a piece of media after finishing it; I created this site as a way to help me form my thoughts and opinions into something both tangible and cohesive, almost like Dumbledore's Pensieve in Harry Potter.

All entries are rated according to my personal enjoyment, not by any objective definition of "quality". Entries are sorted within each tier first by Rating (highest first), then hidden Weight (lowest first), and finally Watch Date (newest first).
