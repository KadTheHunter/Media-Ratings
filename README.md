# Media Ratings

A website to track, arrange and display my ratings and reviews of various pieces of media.

## Why is this not part of Shouting into the Void?

Mainly because it couldn't really be achieved within the confines of the minimal-mistakes jekyll theme (or any jekyll theme really), at least not without substantial effort, and I am an incredibly lazy individual.

Even just frankensteining these files into the existing SitV site would have been too much work, so I chose to simply make it a separate site.

## REEEEE, AI CODE!1!!!!1!

Yes I used generative AI to write the bulk of the HTML/CSS/JS. 

No I do not consider it to be "vibe-coded", particularly given the amount of times it hallucinated or just gave nonsensical answers that even I with my limited knowledge knew was wrong; I subsequently had to fix or figure it out myself far too often.

Furthermore, as I mentioned above: I am an incredibly lazy individual. I also have the stylistic sense of a schizophrenic bag lady; If I hadn't used AI, you would be looking at an eyesore of a website.

And _obviously_ I don't use AI to write the reviews, because 1. I'm not Indian, and 2. that would defeat the entire point of this site.
